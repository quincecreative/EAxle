EDD - Electronical Disconnect Differential

The EDD is used in electrical driven secondary axle of axle split hybrids or battery electric AWD cars. The EDD is located inside the final drive, disconnecting the wheels from the final drive when AWD or hybrid function is not demanded. The disconnecting of the electric drive close to the wheels with almost no rotating parts in the transmission provides lowest drag torque and maximum efficiency gain.
